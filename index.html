Mushroom Farm Layout Planner – Instructions & Best Practices 
• Keep the Compost Hall central for easy access.
• Lab should be adjacent to Compost for quick spawn transfers.
• Incubation rooms need stable 24‑26 °C, 60‑70 % RH.
• Fruiting rooms: 18‑22 °C, 85‑95 % RH, indirect light.
• Maintain a one‑way workflow: Compost → Lab → Incubation → Fruiting.
• Use doors/windows to control airflow and contamination.
• Place fans for gentle air circulation; avoid direct drafts on fruiting.
• Keep the floor grid to 1 m for accurate spacing and cleaning paths.
• Each room contains 5 trays; every tray is divided into 3 sections.
